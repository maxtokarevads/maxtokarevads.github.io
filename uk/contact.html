FAQ

Часті запитання

What's the minimum budget you work with? +
The minimum comfortable ad spend is $1,000/mo per platform. Below that, there isn't enough data to optimize properly. My management fee is separate from your ad budget.
How quickly will I see results? +
Initial results are visible within 2–4 weeks of launch. Full optimization takes 1.5–3 months — algorithms need time to learn. Quick wins (20–30% CPL reduction) often show up in the first month.
Do you charge agency markups on ad spend? +
No. I charge a flat management fee or a percentage of ad budget — no platform kickbacks or hidden markups. Your ad spend goes directly into your ad account, which you own.
What does your reporting look like? +
Weekly report covering key metrics (CPL, ROAS, CPC, CTR, spend), changes made that week, and plan for the next. Larger projects get a real-time dashboard in Looker Studio or Notion.
Can you take over campaigns that are already running? +
Yes — most of my projects start this way. We begin with an audit, I identify what's not working, we agree on priorities, and I take over. Campaigns don't need to stop during the transition.
