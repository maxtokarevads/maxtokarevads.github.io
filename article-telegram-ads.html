Telegram Ads in 2026: Full Setup Guide for E-commerce and B2C

Max Tokarev 📅 June 2026 ⏱ 7 min read
Quick Answer

Telegram Ads (the official platform at ads.telegram.org) places sponsored messages inside public channels — reaching real, engaged subscribers at CPMs often 3–5× lower than Meta. Best for CIS markets, crypto/fintech, online education and e-commerce. For younger audiences, TikTok Ads cover similar CIS demographics. Minimum budget recently dropped to €2, making it accessible for testing. I manage Telegram Ads as part of my multi-platform advertising services.

Most advertisers confuse Telegram Ads with buying posts in channels or using bots. The official Telegram ad platform — ads.telegram.org — is different: it places sponsored messages at the bottom of public channels with 1,000+ subscribers, running on a CPM model.

It's still underused by Western advertisers, which means CPMs of €0.5–€3 for audiences that cost €5–€15 on Meta. That gap is closing, but it's still significant in 2026.

What Telegram Ads Actually Are

Sponsored messages appear at the bottom of a channel's post feed — similar to how promoted tweets appeared in Twitter feeds. They're labeled "Sponsored" and users can dismiss them. The ad contains: text (up to 160 characters), optional button, and link to a channel, bot or external URL.

You're not buying a post in a channel. You're buying CPM access to subscribers of channels in your chosen category or specific channel targets.

Targeting Options in 2026

  • Channel topics — Telegram's own categorization (Technology, Finance, Fashion, News, etc.)
  • Specific channels — target subscribers of named channels (subject to Telegram approval)
  • Language — Russian, English, Ukrainian, Arabic, etc.
  • Geography — country-level targeting
  • Device — iOS, Android, desktop

No interest or behavioral targeting. Telegram doesn't have Facebook-style interest graphs. Your targeting is entirely channel-based. Choose your channel categories carefully — they define your audience.

When Telegram Ads work best alongside other channels like Meta Ads

  • CIS markets (Ukraine, Kazakhstan, Russia, Belarus) — Telegram penetration in these countries is 60–80% of the adult population. No other channel reaches them this cheaply.
  • Crypto and Web3 — Telegram is the native platform for crypto communities. CPMs are higher (€2–5) but audience intent is strong.
  • Online education and courses — especially in Russian/Ukrainian language. Educational channels have highly engaged subscribers.
  • News-adjacent products — VPNs, productivity tools, financial apps — audiences that follow news channels buy these.
  • E-commerce fashion/electronics — works in CIS, emerging in MENA.

Setup: Step by Step

  1. Go to ads.telegram.org and log in with your Telegram account
  2. Create a new ad — write up to 160 characters of text + optional button
  3. Choose targeting: topics, languages, countries
  4. Set CPM bid (start at €1, adjust based on delivery)
  5. Top up your balance (minimum €2, payments via card or crypto)
  6. Submit for review — Telegram moderates ads manually (1–24 hours)

Creative Best Practices

With only 160 characters, every word counts:

  • Lead with the benefit, not the product name
  • Use numbers: "2,000 студентов уже прошли" performs better than generic claims
  • CTA button text: "Попробовать бесплатно" or "Узнать больше" outperform generic "Подробнее"
  • Match the channel's tone — crypto channels expect direct language, news channels expect news-style hooks
  • Test 3–5 creatives simultaneously before scaling

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum budget for Telegram Ads?
The minimum top-up is €2 (recently lowered from €2,000). For meaningful data you need at least €50–100/week on focused channel targeting.

Can Telegram Ads target by country?
Yes — country, language, channel topics and specific channels. No behavioral or interest-based targeting.

What niches work best?
Crypto/Web3, fintech, e-commerce (CIS markets), online education and software tools. CIS markets have the highest penetration and lowest CPMs.
